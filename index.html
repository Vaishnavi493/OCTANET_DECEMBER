"ORGANIC FARMING IS NATURES CLAIM TO FAME"
There are no chemicals in this loving game, Where nature and farmers work hand in hand to grow a healthy land.Organic farming represents a sustainable agricultural approach that emphasizes harmony with nature. This method refrains from using synthetic pesticides, fertilizers, or genetically modified organisms (GMOs). Instead, it relies on natural processes, crop rotation, composting, and biological pest control to maintain soil fertility and manage pests.
